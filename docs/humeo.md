## Audience
- Self-employed business owners.
- Recording video for personal brand; many reasons to do it.
- Saw famous people, looks like easy life, famous too.
- Narrowed down to who has true commercial upside.

## ICP
- Mortuary less needed than coaching obviously, probably good to make a list for this.
- Huge revenue, less likely to be interested than small to medium enterprises. Relatively cheap.

### People who have Downstream Offer
- If you are a coach, make content to bring people into a coaching course (downstream offer).
- Doctor -> marketing for practice.
- Company -> selling product / service.

## Marketing Angle
- Ads perform well in marketing.
- Uses a dataset of a bunch of long-form videos, professionally editing into marketing content.
- 1k targeted > 1 mil views.
- They don't care about social media; they just care about the outcome.
